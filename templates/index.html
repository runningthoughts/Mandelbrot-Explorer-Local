Mandelbrot Fractal Explorer

Presets

Play around with the numbers. The more you zoom in, the more subtle you have to be adjusting X, Y and Zoom levels. Too much and you may end up zooming in on a blank part of the image. Nudges work better until you get a feel for it.

Note that panning and zooming will automatically cause a regeneration of the Mandelbrot fractal. Only use Generate Fractal if you change the values in the form fields. Also note that, at this time, using presets does not change the zoom factor. It is best to manually adjust the values and click Generate Fractal.
